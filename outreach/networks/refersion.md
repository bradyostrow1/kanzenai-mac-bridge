**Time:** 5 min

## After signup — Refersion programs in our vendor space
Refersion has a marketplace at https://refersion.com/marketplace — search:
- "real estate"
- "CRM"
- "email marketing"
- "AI" (broader, but useful for adjacent tools)

Sign up for whatever matches; each approval ID flows back via Refersion's dashboard.

## Notes
